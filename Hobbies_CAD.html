Scrambler for Science Olympiad





For this event, participants designed a device powered by a 1.5kg falling mass to transport an egg along a 9-12m track and stop it as close as possible to a terminal barrier without cracking the egg (which was placed at the front of the vehicle). A mandatory obstacle, a bucket, was placed at the halfway point, and the vehicle had to navigate around it. The device, in its ready-to-launch position, had to fit within 0.75m by 0.75m by 0.5m, and no electronics or remote controls could be used. The goal was to use gravitational potential energy to move the egg safely and accurately to the end.
