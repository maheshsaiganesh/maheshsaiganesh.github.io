One Plant Many Families

I have volunteered with OPMF(One Plant Many Families) for almost two years now(also serving as a leader this past year). We strive to serve out community, one plant at a time; with our main goal being to alleviate the drastic food shortages in our communities. We grow our own fresh produce while simultaneously collecting donations from willing neighbours/markets to donate to the ‘Food for the Journey Project’(a local group that services the Dayton community).
