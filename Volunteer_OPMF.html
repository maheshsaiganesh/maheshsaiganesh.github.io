One Plant Many Families

I've been a volunteer with & early member of One Plant Many Families(OPMF) for four years, serving as a leader for the past three. Our mission is to address the pressing issue of food insecurity in our community by partnering with local food banks to host free food drives. Each year, we grow fresh produce and gather donations from supportive neighbors and local markets. All contributions go directly to our partner, The Food for the Journey Project, a mobile food bank that has been fighting hunger in the Dayton area for over a decade.
