One Plant Many Families

I have volunteered with One Plant Many Families(OPMF) for almost three years now (as a leader these past 2 years). We strive to alleviate the drastic food shortages amongst our community by partnering with local food banks to host food and donation drives. Every year, we grow our own fresh produce while collecting donations from willing neighbours/local markets that is then donated to our partner, The Food For The Journey Project(a local Food Bank that has been providing a mobile response to hunger in the Dayton community for over a decade now).
