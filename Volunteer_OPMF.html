One Plant Many Families

I joined One Plant Many Families (OPMF) early in high school and remained an active volunteer throughout all four years, serving as a leader for three. Our mission is to combat food insecurity in our community by partnering with local food banks to organize free food drives. Each year, we grow fresh produce and collect donations from neighbors and local markets, with all contributions going directly to our partner, The Food for the Journey Project—a mobile food bank that has served the Dayton area for over a decade. Through this work, I’ve gained meaningful experience in leadership, community engagement, and the importance of direct action in addressing hunger.
