Washington-Centerville Teen Advisory Board

For five years(8th grade through high school), I volunteered with TAB(the Washington-Centerville Teen Advisory Board). Filled with innovative and energetic teens from Grades 6 through 12, TAB members helped shape the future of the Library by developing and hosting it's programs & services, promoting library materials, and encouraging literacy amongst the youth in our community. We met twice a month to have a blast creating new ways to make the library increasingly "teen friendly."
