Washington-Centerville Teen Advisory Board

I have volunteered with TAB(the Washington-Centerville Teen Advisory Board) for almost two years now. TAB is a group of innovative and energetic teens in Grades 6 to 12, who help shape the future of the Library by developing programs and services, recommending materials for purchase, and promoting the Library to other teens. TAB meets twice a month and has a blast creating new ways to make the Library "teen friendly."
